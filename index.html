I am a third-year undergraduate student at Northwestern University.

I have a wide range of interests, but recently I have been particularly interested in Low Temperature Physics, Superconductivity, and Quantum Computing. I also have experience in Theoretical Astrophysics, Applied Math, and Computational Physics. Currently, my research is in characterization of material losses in superconducting circuits, with direct applications in quantum computing.

You can learn more about me in the page work of this website, my resume or my CV. If you are feeling particularly invested in this task, I also have a few blog posts and an about section.
